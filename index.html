A Letter For You

From me, to you - on our first anniversary

My dearest,

One year. 365 days. And somehow, it still doesn't feel like enough time to put into words how much you mean to me.

I still remember the very first time we talked - "bff vreen" and "plastic mo boi" - and how something so silly turned into something so beautiful. I didn't know then that I was talking to the person who would become my favorite part of every single day.

Every time Mundo plays, I think of you. You are my mundo - my whole world.

You make me laugh until my stomach hurts. You're there in the late night calls, in the random memes, in the "may dropper kayo?" moments that somehow turned into forever kind of feelings.

Thank you for choosing me. Thank you for staying. Thank you for every stupid joke and every quiet moment and every time you made me feel like I was exactly where I was supposed to be.

I want to grow old with you. I want a thousand more anniversaries, a thousand more reasons to fall in love with you all over again.

Happy 1st anniversary, my love.

Yours, always and forever
